### Who is the end user?

### What does the user want?

### What frustrates the user?

### What does the publisher want?

### What problems does publisher have now?








































































    End users are readers, students interested in discovering short stories based on themes, emotions, or situations.

    Users want to quickly find stories relevant to their interests and understand what the story is about before reading it.

    Users are frustrated when keyword search fails and when they cannot quickly understand story content.

    The publisher wants readers to easily discover stories and engage with their content through modern semantic search.

    The publisher struggles with making stories discoverable using traditional keyword search.
